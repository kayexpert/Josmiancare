Welcome to Josmiancare

Josmiancare Corporation provides 24/7 trauma-informed residential care for children and youth in Ottawa. Our team creates a safe, structured, and nurturing space where every young person is seen, heard, and supported.

Mission-aligned, clinically guided

Our continuum of care blends compassionate routines with clinical partnerships. Youth receive individualized plans, consistent communication, and a sense of belonging that extends beyond our homes into school, community, and future transitions.

  • Individual care plans tailored to each youth's needs and goals
  • Collaborative approach with families, agencies, and community partners
  • Purposeful routines that emphasize safety, voice, and cultural connection
