About Josmiancare

Compassionate, Professional, and Evidence-Based Care

Welcome to Josmiancare

Josmiancare Corporation provides 24/7 trauma-informed residential care for children and youth in Ottawa. Our team creates a safe, structured, and nurturing space where every young person is seen, heard, and supported.

Mission-aligned, clinically guided

Our continuum of care blends compassionate routines with clinical partnerships. Youth receive individualized plans, consistent communication, and a sense of belonging that extends beyond our homes into school, community, and future transitions.

  • Individual care plans tailored to each youth's needs and goals
  • Collaborative approach with families, agencies, and community partners
  • Purposeful routines that emphasize safety, voice, and cultural connection

Our Mission & Approach

At Josmiancare, we offer a stable, home-like environment staffed by qualified child and youth workers and supervisory personnel. Within this setting, youth receive consistent care, educational support, life-skills training, and therapeutic services that promote independence, resilience, and community reintegration.

The Group Home Program is an integral part of Josmiancare's continuum of care, complementing foster care, family support, and clinical outreach. We ensure continuity of care through coordinated case management, multidisciplinary collaboration, and transparent communication.

Our Core Principles

Our approach is grounded in the following core principles:

Child-Centred Care

Rights-based care that respects the individuality of every child.

Safety & Well-Being

Prioritizing the safety, well-being, and stability of our residents.

Trauma-Informed

Culturally responsive practice that understands and addresses trauma.

Supportive Interventions

Least intrusive, most supportive interventions to guide growth.

Collaboration

Working together with families, agencies, and the community.

Accountability

Transparent communication and commitment to the best outcomes.

"Through these guiding values, Josmiancare Corporation remains committed to providing compassionate, professional, and evidence-based care that empowers youth to heal, grow, and build brighter futures."
